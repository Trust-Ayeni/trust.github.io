Facial Recognition to detect cheating

Facial recognition to detect cheating refers to the use of technology that analyzes and identifies individuals' facial features to detect any irregularities or fraudulent behavior. This technology is often used in educational or professional settings to prevent cheating during exams or assessments. When a person is taking an exam, their facial features are captured and analyzed by a facial recognition system. The system can then compare the data against pre-existing data to identify any discrepancies or anomalies that may indicate cheating. For example, if the system detects that the person is looking away from their computer screen for an extended period or interacting with another individual, it may raise an alert that cheating is occurring.
